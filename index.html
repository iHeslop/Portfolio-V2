Architecture

Winter Sports World
2021-2023 // Environa Studio // Private

Winter Sports World is completely unique in both design and purpose, and smart design principles and innovation have been used to create an incredible architectural statement. The building is a world-first in sustainable innovation.

Norfolk Residences
2021-2022 // Environa Studio // Private

Norfolk Residences presents a rare collection of just 10 luxurious apartments and townhomes, meticulously designed and beautifully appointed for downsizers and those seeking to enjoy the lifestyle of exclusive North Avalon. The design provides deluxe beachside seniors living.

Narrabeen
2020-2022 // Environa Studio // Private

Beach St, Narrabeen is an innovative modern take on deluxe sea-side design. The use of mutliple pavilions to differentiate between multiple private and public spaces creates a unique and special home that looks to maximise on the natural surrounding beauty.

Elizabeth Bay
2020-2022 // Environa Studio // Private

An innovative design that looks to integrate key worker housing into one of Sydney's richest and affluent areas. The goal of the design was to create quality inner-city housing for key-workers within Sydneys CBD. The building itself serves as an extension to the heritage listed home already situated on the site.

Coogee
2020-2021 // Environa Studio // Private

Mount St, Coogee is a high-quality, modern apartment building which looks to provide deluxe living using modern design principles to create diverse living spaces. The use of sustainable materials and detailing creates apartments that are spacious and curated to the user.

Portman Street
2020-2021 // Environa Studio // Private

A deluxe, high quality dual-townhouse in the inner-west of Sydney. The design aims to fit seamlessly into the current surroundings, whilst providing a modern and innovative approach to traditional design. The use of modern and sustainable materials, as well as the incorporation of light wells creates spaces that are inviting and welcoming.

Transitions
2019 // University of Sydney // Portfolio

Transitions looks to continue the natural gradient and transition between the architecture of the city and the landscape of the Domain. The theatre uses geometric and parametric design intertwined with aesthetic and organic form to act as a mediator between the city and domain.

Urban Oasis
2019 // University of Sydney // Portfolio

Urban Oasis is a large voluminous catenary swimming centre that aims to create seemless transitions between closed and open spaces as well as a full acoustic, visual and sensual experience of water with a beautiful engagement with timber as a structural material.

Stanmore Housing
2018 // University of Sydney // Portfolio

This studio project focussed on the creation of layers between spaces, with access to sunlight, privacy and simplicity of spatial arrangement central to the design process. The housing project has been designed to facilitate middle-aged homeless womens growth and development in a safe and comfortable environment.
